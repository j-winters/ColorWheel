You will be presented with a circle of 32 colors. Your task is to click on all colors that could be described by the color word. Each color word will be displayed within the circle (e.g., black). Once you have selected a color from the circle it will appear in one of the squares above the color circle. To remove a color from your selection, simply click on the square containing that color. When you have finished your selection of color(s), click on Next to move onto the next word.
Please click anywhere on the screen to start the task.
