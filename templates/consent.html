Consent Form

This study is concerned with color words. You will first be shown a wheel of 32 colors and a specific color word (e.g., black). Your goal is to select which color(s) you associate with that word by clicking on the color. Once you have clicked on the relevant color(s), click on the Next button to generate the next color word. You can select the same color(s) for different color words. These steps will keep repeating until you have associated all of the colors with at least one color word. Overall, the task will take approximately 5 minutes of your time.
